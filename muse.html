Our Events

Being lovers of words, we keep organizing meets and events to broaden people's perspectives and to help budding writers shine. Some such events include - online events like - word prompt, micro tales etc, book club meets where people meet to discuss great pieces of literature. Every meet includes crosswords, quizzes and other fun games based on a particular theme. If like us, you also believe you can concur the world with your words, do join us
